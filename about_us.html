A Trusted Name in Steel Construction

KR Steel Structure Limited is a leading steel structure and pre-engineered building manufacturer in Bangladesh, providing complete solutions from design and engineering to fabrication and installation. Backed by modern manufacturing facilities and an experienced workforce, we deliver safe, durable, and cost-effective steel structures for industrial and commercial projects across the country.
